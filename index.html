Case Studies

Case Study 01 · Destination Content Positioning

Situation A nature-focused destination page near Nha Trang needed stronger short-form content that could stop viewers quickly while staying true to the location’s real experience.
Task Reframe generic “beautiful location” messaging into a stronger audience-driven hook that sells emotional escape, not just scenery.
Action Shifted the message from descriptive visual language to a more psychological angle: “a place to disappear from the city for a while,” then paired it with clear value framing and more practical CTA language.
Result Helped build a more differentiated content direction centered on reset, escape, and mood change — a stronger foundation for future travel-education style content.

Case Study 02 · Hospitality Content Optimization

Situation A venue page with mixed offerings required more disciplined content execution while operations were under pressure and customer expectations were sensitive.
Task Keep brand visibility alive without overpromising what the in-person experience could not consistently support.
Action Recommended content that stays realistic, avoids inflated claims, and focuses on atmosphere, use-case, and what is genuinely still strong in the venue ecosystem.
Result Created a safer content direction that protects brand trust while preserving page activity and reducing the risk of audience disappointment caused by over-marketing.

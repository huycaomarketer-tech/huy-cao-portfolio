About Huy Cao

Marketing Executive focused on content, social media, and audience-oriented storytelling, with academic achievements and early professional experience built around real execution.

My name is Huy Cao, and I am a Marketing graduate from Hoa Sen University. I started building my path through university projects, communication competitions, and research work, then transitioned into hands-on content and social media roles after graduation. What shaped me most was not only learning marketing theories in class, but seeing how content actually affects audience attention, engagement, and business results in real environments.

During university, I actively joined academic and communication activities that helped me think beyond “posting for the sake of posting.” One milestone I am especially proud of was becoming a semi-finalist at the Euréka Scientific Research Awards 2025 with a project about Gen Z’s awareness and behavior toward greenwashing in marketing. That experience trained me to look deeper into consumer insight, behavior patterns, and the gap between what brands say and what audiences truly believe.

From university projects to real execution

After graduation, I moved into practical marketing roles where I worked on content planning, page management, short-form videos, and social media execution. I learned how to turn broad brand requests into usable content directions, how to write for actual platform behavior, and how to keep content relevant even when business conditions are not ideal. I am especially interested in hospitality, F&B, lifestyle, and destination-related brands because these categories require both emotional storytelling and commercial awareness.

My professional experience includes internships and operational content work where I produced articles, managed social platforms, coordinated visual direction, and worked with social-first formats such as TikTok and Facebook. Across these roles, I became more confident in understanding what makes content stop people, what makes them care, and what makes them take action.

What I am building next

I do not see myself as only a writer or only a page operator. I am building toward becoming a strong Marketing Executive who understands both strategy and execution: someone who can read audience psychology, shape content systems, and support a brand’s communication in a way that is practical, consistent, and commercially useful. My goal is to keep growing in environments where content is not treated as decoration, but as a real part of how brands stay alive and relevant.

This page was created as part of my professional portfolio to present not only what I have done, but how I think about marketing work.
